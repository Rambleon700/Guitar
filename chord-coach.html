Chord Coach Live

Play along. Get graded in real time.

Build a chord chart for any song, set the tempo, and Chord Coach scrolls it toward a playhead like a practice-along track — listening through your mic to tell you if you actually hit each chord.

1. Pick a chart

#ChordBeats
Save this chart as a file to your local library, or load a previously saved chart.

Or transcribe from an audio file

Drop in an MP3/WAV of the song you're practicing. This runs entirely in your browser — no upload, no external service. It decodes the file, builds a chromagram, and pattern-matches chords frame by frame, then fills in the chart above so you can review and fix it.

2. Check your mic

Chord Coach listens for the pitch classes in each chord as you strum. You can also run it with the mic off — you'll still get the scrolling chart and click track.

    Chord Coach grades what your mic actually hears — the pitch classes present in each chord you strum — against the chart you built. It isn't listening to a recording and figuring out the song's chords for you (reliable automatic chord recognition from raw audio isn't something a self-contained browser tool can do well); you tell it the chart once, the way any practice-along tool does, and from then on it's real-time listening and grading.
